Our Story

Founded in 2020, StudentFunding.co.za began as a small initiative to help South African students navigate the complex world of educational funding and career opportunities. What started as a simple directory has grown into the most comprehensive bursary and job platform for students in South Africa.

We understand the challenges students face in accessing quality education and launching their careers. Our platform was created to bridge the gap between talented students and the organizations that want to support them.

Our Mission

To democratize access to education and career opportunities by providing a centralized, up-to-date platform that connects students with funding sources and employers across South Africa.

Our Vision

A South Africa where no deserving student misses out on education due to financial constraints, and where graduates seamlessly transition into meaningful careers.

Our Values

Accessibility

We believe education funding information should be freely available to all students.

Integrity

We verify all opportunities and maintain strict editorial standards.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform to better serve students.

Community

We foster connections between students, funders, and employers.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of students find funding and career opportunities. Our platform lists hundreds of bursaries and jobs annually, with a success rate that speaks for itself:

  • Over 50,000 monthly visitors
  • 500+ bursaries listed annually
  • 1,000+ job opportunities posted each year
  • Partnerships with major universities and corporations
